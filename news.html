Winter Wonderland

December 19, 2022

Once a year the Good Tidings warehouse is transformed into a Winter Wonderland, where 25 of our fourth grade and younger kids could pick out holiday gifts. Attending the Winter Wonderland has become a tradition for Play Marin kids and it warms our hearts to be invited every year since 2018.

Holiday Heroes

December 3, 2022

Nearly 50 kids in K-8th grade got to attend Holiday Heroes in San Francisco where they participated in crafts, decorated cookies, got snow globes, received gifts, and met local heroes including some 49er cheerleaders.

San Diego State vs Stanford

November 15, 2022

Play Marin arranged a bus and got tickets to travel to Stanford University to watch Marin City’s own Darrion Trammell play basketball for San Diego State University.

Over 75 kids and parents came to root for Darrion who is an inspiration that with hard work and practice you can achieve your dreams.

Wakanda Forever

November 11, 2022

A good time taking this group to see the premier of Wakanda Forever. Thanks to Felecia Gaston and Target for organizing this wonderful opportunity. Partnership & community is important.

Walkathon

October 22, 2022

The Play Marin Walkathon was a wonderful event supported by the community raising over $12,000. We had student volunteers from a variety of high schools come out and support our walkers, offer face painting, and playing with the kids.

We even had one high school walker, Mac Kayser, who personally raised $650! Read his story.
